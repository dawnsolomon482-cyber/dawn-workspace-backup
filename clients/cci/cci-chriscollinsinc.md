### Kailan Humingi
- After a big win / client shares results
- After Month 3 of coaching
- After a positive call or email feedback
- After completing a coaching milestone

### Review Link Setup
1. Go to business.google.com
2. Left menu → Ask for reviews
3. Copy the link
4. Shorten via bit.ly

### Email Template
> **Subject: Quick favor — would mean a lot to us**
>
> Hey [First Name],
>
> Seeing the results you've achieved — [mention specific result] — is exactly why we do what we do.
>
> If you've found our coaching valuable, would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? It takes less than 2 minutes and helps other dealerships find us.
>
> [GBP REVIEW LINK]
>
> Thanks,
> [Coach Name]
> Chris Collins Inc.

### Text/SMS Template
> "Hey [Name], loved hearing about your results this quarter. Would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? [link] — takes 2 mins. Really appreciate it!"

### Follow-up (5 days, no response)
> "Hey [Name] — just following up. No worries if busy, but if you get a chance, a quick Google review would mean a lot to the team. [link]"

### Response Template (After Review)
> "Thank you [Name]! Working with [Dealership Name] has been incredible — seeing your team achieve [specific result] is exactly what drives us. Looking forward to continued success together!"

### Review Targets
- Month 1: 5 new reviews
- Month 3: 20+ reviews
- Ongoing: 2-4 new reviews per month
