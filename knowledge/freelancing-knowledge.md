## Finding Clients

### Best Platforms for SEO/WordPress/Automation Freelancers
- **Upwork** — best for long-term contracts, hourly or fixed
- **Fiverr** — best for packaged services (e.g., "I will set up your WordPress site")
- **Facebook Groups** — PH-based: "Trabaho PH", "Filipino Virtual Assistants", "Online Jobs PH"
- **LinkedIn** — direct outreach to small business owners
- **OnlineJobs.ph** — best PH platform for finding foreign clients (US, AU, UK)
- **Contra** — no fees, growing platform for freelancers

### Cold Outreach Strategy
1. Find local businesses with bad SEO or no website
2. Do a quick audit (check speed, GSC, keywords)
3. Send a short, specific message: "I found 3 issues on your site that are hurting your Google ranking. Can I show you?"
4. Offer a free audit call, not a free job
5. Follow up once after 3 days

## Client Onboarding Checklist

- [ ] Signed contract (use HelloSign or DocuSign free tier)
- [ ] 50% deposit before starting
- [ ] Access: website admin, GSC, GA4, hosting
- [ ] Agreed deliverables and timeline in writing
- [ ] Communication channel set (Telegram, email, or Slack)

---

## Retaining Clients (Long-Term Income)

- Always deliver a monthly report (even a simple one)
- Offer maintenance packages after project ends
- Upsell: finish WordPress site → offer SEO → offer GHL automation
- Check in monthly even when not working — short "how's the site doing?" message
- Ask for referrals after a successful project
