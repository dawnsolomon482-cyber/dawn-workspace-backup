## Core Philosophy

- SEO is about **answering questions customers are already asking** — not tricking Google
- SEO = **Content + Optimization + Consistency**
- **Bottom of funnel (BOFU) first** — target buyers, not browsers
- Build domain authority first, then go after competitive keywords
- Systems + SOPs make SEO repeatable and scalable
- E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust) is the ranking standard
- Long-term sustainable growth over quick wins

---

## Strategy 1: The Avalanche Method (HeyTony)

Start with low-competition keywords → build authority → go after harder keywords.

1. Find low-hanging fruit keywords (low volume, low competition)
2. Create quality content targeting those keywords
3. Build traffic and domain authority
4. Use that authority to rank for harder, high-volume keywords

---

## Strategy 2: Topical Authority (HeyTony + Connor Gillivan)

Become the **most complete, trustworthy resource on one specific topic.**

- Answer ALL questions around a topic — better than anyone else
- Build Hub-and-Spoke clusters: pillar page (hub) + supporting blog posts (spokes)
- Every spoke links back to hub, hub links to all spokes
- Don't spread across many niches — go deep on one topic
- Semantic SEO: build content that mirrors how real people think and search
- Google sees well-connected, helpful pages as an "answer hub"

---

## Strategy 3: The 4 Pillars of SEO (Connor Gillivan)

### Pillar 1: Content & Upgrades
- Write for your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) — not generic audiences
- Focus on BOFU keywords: "best X for Y", comparison pages, vs. pages, how-to
- Publish 5-10+ keyword-focused articles/pages per month
- Find pages ranking #10-50 → upgrade content → push to page 1 (fastest wins)

### Pillar 2: Backlinks
- Build backlinks from reputable, relevant sources
- Methods: content partnerships, podcast appearances, HARO, niche edits, expert outreach
- Quality over quantity — relevant industry links beat random ones

### Pillar 3: CRO & UX
- Turn traffic into leads — SEO alone isn't enough
- Optimize landing pages, CTAs, and user flow
- Content must convert, not just rank

### Pillar 4: Analytics
- Review Google Search Console + GA4 regularly
- Find what's working → double down
- Find pages slipping → upgrade before they drop further

## Keyword Research Process

**Tier 1 (Do first):** High intent + Low KD + Decent search volume
**Tier 2:** Medium intent + Medium KD (after domain authority builds)
**Tier 3:** High volume + High competition (only after authority established)

Steps:
1. Analyze competitors — what are they ranking for, where are the gaps?
2. Find low-competition, question-based keywords first
3. Do keyword research BEFORE writing any content
4. Prioritize: High intent + Low KD + Good search volume = TOP PRIORITY

**Biggest mistake:** Not using the target keyword on the actual page

## Content Strategy

- Blog minimum 1x/month (small business), ideally weekly
- **BIG 5 Framework:** Answer questions people ask before they buy
- BOFU content: "best [tool] for [niche]", "[X] vs [Y]", "how to [specific problem]"
- Update old blog posts — fastest way to boost traffic (refresh titles, stats, visuals, keywords)
- Pages ranking #10-50 are the fastest wins — upgrade them first
- 100% human-written and edited — no pure AI content
- Publish 2 new content pieces in week 1 of any new engagement

---

## On-Page SEO Checklist

- Target keyword in: page title, H1, URL, first paragraph, meta description
- Title tag: under 60 characters, keyword + power word
- Meta description: 120-155 characters, includes CTA
- Short, keyword-friendly URL slugs
- No duplicate content across pages
- No keyword stuffing — match competitor keyword density

---

## Internal Linking Rules

- 3-5 internal links per page as baseline
- Always link from old content to new blog posts
- Use descriptive, relevant anchor text — never generic "click here"
- Links must feel natural — don't force them
- Hub links to all spokes, every spoke links back to hub

---

## Local SEO Checklist

- Business name, address, phone in footer of every page
- Google Business Profile fully updated: name, address, phone, description, services, photos
- More Google reviews = better local rankings — actively collect reviews
- NAP consistency across all directories

---

## Technical SEO

- robots.txt properly configured
- Page speed optimized — mobile score under 60 is critical
- Google Search Console errors resolved
- No duplicate content
- Clean internal linking structure
- Crawlability verified

---

## GEO — Generative Engine Optimization (2025+)

Rank in AI platforms: ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overview

- GEO is wide open in 2025 — easier to win now than in 2026
- E-E-A-T applies to AI rankings just like Google
- Structured, expert content beats generic AI-written content
- Treat AI citations like backlinks — be the source AI recommends
- Proof: ranking in ChatGPT → 25+ leads → $10,000+ revenue (TrioSEO case study)

## First 90 Days Process (New Client/Website)

1. Keyword research + competitor gap analysis
2. Publish 2 content pieces in week 1
3. Consistent weekly content publishing
4. On-page optimization of existing pages
5. Build domain trust and authority
6. Technical SEO fixes
7. Internal linking structure
8. Target harder keywords with built authority

## Tools

- Google Search Console — track rankings, impressions, clicks (free, always use)
- Ahrefs — competitor research, keyword gaps, backlink analysis
- SEMrush — keyword research, content gaps
- Surfer SEO — on-page content scoring and optimization
- SEOwind — article outlines and content briefs
- GA4 — traffic and conversion analytics
- Google Business Profile — local SEO (free, must be fully set up)
