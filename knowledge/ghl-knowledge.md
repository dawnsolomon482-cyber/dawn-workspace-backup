## What is GoHighLevel?
- All-in-one marketing/CRM platform for agencies and local businesses
- Replaces: ClickFunnels, Mailchimp, Calendly, Twilio, ManyChat, and more
- White-label SaaS — agencies resell it under their own brand

## Core GHL Features

### CRM & Pipeline
- Contact management with tags, custom fields, smart lists
- Sales pipelines with drag-and-drop stages
- Opportunity tracking and deal value

### Communication Channels
- SMS (Twilio integration)
- Email (built-in SMTP or Mailgun)
- Voicemail drops
- Facebook & Instagram DMs
- Google Business Messages
- WhatsApp (native integration available)
- Live chat widget

### Automation (Workflows)
- Trigger-based automations (form submit, tag added, appointment booked, etc.)
- AI-powered workflows (AI chat, AI appointment booking)
- Multi-step drip campaigns
- Webhook and HTTP request actions (connect to n8n, Make, Zapier)

### Funnels & Websites
- Drag-and-drop funnel/website builder
- Order forms, upsells, downsells
- Membership sites and courses

### Appointments & Calendars
- Round-robin, team, class booking
- Google/Outlook calendar sync
- Auto-reminders via SMS/email

### Reputation Management
- Auto-request Google/Facebook reviews after appointment
- Monitor and respond to reviews inside GHL

### Reporting & Analytics
- Ad reporting (Facebook, Google)
- Funnel conversion stats
- Call tracking and recording
