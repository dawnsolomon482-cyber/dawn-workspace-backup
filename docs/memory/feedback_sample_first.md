Always present a single sample/prototype to the boss (Kier) for approval before executing the full batch — whether that's uploading all 15 emails to GHL, generating all files, or any other bulk operation.

**Why:** Avoids wasting time, tokens, and effort if the boss wants changes. One sample is cheap to fix; fixing 15 is not.

**How to apply:** Any time a task involves creating multiple similar items (emails, templates, pages, etc.) — build 1 first, show it, wait for approval, then do the rest.
